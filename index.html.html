About Me

Based In Muzaffarnagar, UP

Hey, I'm Ayman Naved — a video editor obsessed with the art of the cut. I got into editing about a year ago after spending too many hours watching YouTube and thinking "I could make that better." Turns out, I could.

I specialize in YouTube content, short-form social videos, and anything that needs to be fast, punchy, and retain attention in a world with a 3-second scroll threshold. I care about pacing, story, and making every second earn its place on the timeline.

Currently open to freelance projects, long-term creator partnerships, and collaborations that push me to grow. If you have footage, I have ideas.
